CMD Properties

In order for SD-Security to display the screens correctly, the correct window size needs to be set. You probably won't need to do this, though some PCs may need to do this:

To Use Original Font (Raster):

  1. Open SD-Security
  2. Right click the title bar
  3. Click Defaults> Font Tab
  4. Click Raster Fonts > 8x12
  5. Click OK
  6. Right click the title bar
  7. Click properties, font tab
  8. Click Raster Fonts > 8x12
  9. Click OK

To Use Updated Font (Lucida Console):

1.   Open SD-Security

2.   Right click the title bar

3.   Click Defaults> Font Tab

4.   Click Lucida Console > 13

5.   Click OK

6.   Right click the title bar

7.   Click properties, font tab

8.   Click Lucida Console > 13

9.   Click OK
